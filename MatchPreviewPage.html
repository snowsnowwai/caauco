How to use QuickDraw!

Quick start guide and walkthrough

Hello There and welcome to Quick Draw!

How to Use:

For clients the main page is the program’s primary matching function as you are given a whole gallery of our registered artists who are ready to work!

  1. Use the arrow at the bottom of the cards to move through the gallery of talented artists.
  2. When you find one you’re interested in, tap to reveal their artist card.
  3. To request or get into contact with them, tap on the message button in the card or the request button to where a pop up will appear and will give you an artist menu for you to choose your specific needs!
  4. On the top task bar will be our messaging system where you can review your chats with the artists you contact!
